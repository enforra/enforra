# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected behavior

Be respectful, constructive, and direct. Assume good intent, accept feedback gracefully, and focus technical disagreement on ideas and evidence.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, personal attacks, and sustained disruption are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior violates this code of conduct.
